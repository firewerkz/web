Practice2 1.02 Released for macOS

Published June 22, 2021 #Practice2 #Practice #macOS

Version 1.02 of Practice2, was approved and released for macOS today

What’s new

  • Added introduction replay option in setting
  • Fixed a few bugs

Use split screen to follow along with another app, like a song book, or guitar tabs
