TimeToGo Version 1.1 Released for Android

Published June 21, 2021 #TimeToGo #TtG #Android

Version 1.1 of TimeToGo, TtG, was approved and released for Android today.

Simple Time to Go App. Calculates the weeks and days from today to a date in the future. Shows the end date for a given numbers of weeks from today. Select an inspiring picture to remind yourself of your goal. Simple.
